## Style
- light mode only
- typography-first (Newneek 참고)
- no cards with gradients
- no glassmorphism
- no decorative illustrations

## Voice
- 정치 정보를 쉽고 담담하게
- 선동적 표현 금지
- 진보/보수 외 임의 강조색 추가 금지

## Forbidden Patterns
- purple-to-blue gradients
- gradient text
- glassmorphism
- bounce/elastic easing
- nested cards
- rounded-square icon tiles above headings
- 3D abstract illustrations
